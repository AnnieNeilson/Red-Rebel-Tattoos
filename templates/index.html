Welcome To Red Rebel Tattoos

We are a team of professional tattooists, together we have over 100 years in tattooing experience!

You can use our booking form to arrange a consultation with a specific tattooist, if you have someone in mind!

We pride ourselves on being a 5 star local health authority registered studio. We carry out all tattooing to the highest standard.
